---
title: Granola
---

## Ingredients

- `660g` rolled oats
- `75g` wheat germ
- `7g` salt
- `5g` cinnamon
- `140g` olive oil
- `160g` maple syrup
- vanilla
- ~`150g` nuts (pecans and/or walnuts)
- `75g` quinoa (optional, swapped in for some oats)

## Notes
Mix oats, wheat germ, salt, cinn in a big bowl (and quinoa, if using). Whisk wet ingredients together and then stir well into grains. Chop nuts and add them last.

Spread over 2 baking trays. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes. Watch for burning; rotate if needed. Don't stir. Allow to cool before breaking up.
