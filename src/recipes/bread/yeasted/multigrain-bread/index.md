---
title: Multigrain Bread
---

## Ingredients

- `100g` starter
- `295g` water (soaking liquid)
- `280g` white flour
- `120g` whole-wheat flour
- `40g` rolled oats (soak first)
- `40g` rolled barley (soak first)
- `40g` rolled buckwheat groats (soak first)
- `14g` salt
- `3g` yeast
- `15g` honey
- `15g` oats (for sprinkling on top before baking)

## Notes
Can mix and match with the grains. Add them up to 120g.
