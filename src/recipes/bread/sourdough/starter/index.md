---
title: Starter warm-up process
---

## Ingredients
Initially:
- `50g` starter
- `100g` water (90-95˚)
- `100g` white flour

Give it 8-12 hours. To the bowl, add:
- `100g` water (90-95˚)
- `100g` white flour

Give it overnight. To a fresh bowl, add:
- `50g` newly hot starter
- `100g` water (90-95˚)
- `100g` white flour

## Notes
Ken calls for 50-60g starter, depending on air temps.
