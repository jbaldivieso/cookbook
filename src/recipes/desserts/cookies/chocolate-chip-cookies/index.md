---
title: Chocolate Chip Cookies
---

## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup (2 sticks) butter, softened
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- ¾ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 cups chocolate chips

## Equipment

- Large mixing bowl
- Electric mixer
- Baking sheets
- Parchment paper

## Instructions

### Prepare the Dough

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl
3. Beat butter and both sugars until creamy
4. Add eggs and vanilla, beat well
5. Gradually blend in flour mixture
6. Stir in chocolate chips

### Bake

1. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto parchment-lined baking sheets
2. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown
3. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes
4. Remove to wire racks to cool completely

## Storage

Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 week.

## Tips

- For chewier cookies, slightly underbake them
- Chill dough for 30 minutes before baking for thicker cookies
- Mix in chopped nuts for extra texture
